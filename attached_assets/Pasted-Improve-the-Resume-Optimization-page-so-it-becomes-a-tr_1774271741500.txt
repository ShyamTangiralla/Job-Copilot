   - Highlight keywords still missing from the tailored resume in red
3. In the AI-Tailored Resume panel:
   - Highlight matched inserted keywords in green
   - Optionally mark still-missing target keywords in a small “Still Missing” section above the resume
4. Keep the ATS score summary at the top, but show:
   - Before score
   - After score
